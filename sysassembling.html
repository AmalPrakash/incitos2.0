SYSTEM ASSEMBLING COMPETITION

SYSTEM ASSEMBLING

Do you have the knowledge about computer hardware?
Are you confident in assembling a PC from disassembled components?



Join us at INCITOS 2.0 for SYSTEM ASSEMBLING COMPETITION where you'll be doing step by step assembling and building of a PC.


RULES AND REGULATIONS

Participants must have the following minimum skills:
- A good understanding of computer system hardware components
- Skills in assembling a computer system and performing basic configuration
- The participants must proceed to their individual work space allotted to them to start the actual assembly and performing the required basic configuration.
- Jolting, banging or mishandling of any of the PC part is strictly not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
- After the assembly, the participant must ensure that the casing is in place and all screws
are tightened before turning on the power.
- Participants will not be allowed to touch their allocated PC after the allotted time is over. No extra time will be provided in any case.
- Registered participants should complete the preliminary round.
- Mobile phones should be switched off during the competition.
- Participants must communicate in a way that will not disturb other Participants.
- Participants may not leave the competition without a valid reason, while the competition is in progress.
